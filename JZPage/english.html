ABOUT ME

JACINTO ZAITZEWSKY WHO AM I

My full name is Jacinto Igor Zaitzewsky Micales and I am 21 years old. I was born and raised in Stockholm, Sweden and I am currently taking a Bachelor's degree in Information Systems at Örebro University.

ÖREBRO UNIVERSITY INFORMATION SYSTEMS

I started studying at Örebro University since fall 2013. Ever since then I have together with my classmates developed business systems in Java, built databases in SQL, created websites with HTML/CSS and Javascript (and jQuery), a podcastapp in C# and a dating website in ASP.Net with MVC. Aside from strictly coding we have held our own SCRUM-projects with Extreme Programming.

SEMESTER ABROAD SAN DIEGO STATE UNIVERSITY

On January 6th 2015 I boarded a plane heading to San Diego to start my semester abroad at San Diego State University. During the six months I stayed in the United States I grew as a person thanks to meeting people from different cultures, facing new challenges and having the opportunity to stay in a new environment.

During my semester in San Diego I took a class called "Social, Legal and Ethical Issues in Computer Science", where concepts like "The Naked Future", "The Internet of Things" and "The Singularity" were discussed. The class focused on issues such as; when will humanity create true Artificial Intelligence? How do you teach a robot right from wrong - and how do you really define what is right and what is wrong? What is telemetry? How will the future unfold when the line between private and public is slowly getting thinner and thinner?
